      FAQ & Contact

      How does visa data work?

      AeroLogic uses the open-source Passport Index dataset, refreshed every 12 hours. Data reflects general visa policies; always verify with the destination embassy before travel.

      How accurate is the data?

      Visa policies change frequently. AeroLogic provides guidance only and does not constitute legal or travel advice. Verify requirements directly with embassies or consulates.

      Can I add multiple travel documents?

      Yes. Use the Documents section above or the + button on the globe to add passports, visas, OCI cards, residence permits, and more. AeroLogic will show the best entry option across all your documents.

      Is my data stored securely?

      Your account and documents are stored in Supabase with row-level security — only you can access your data. We never sell or share your information.
